make it play faster than 2x:
	-Go to the video.
	-Press CTRL + SHIFT + J
	-Copy and paste the following:
		document.getElementsByTagName("video")[0].playbackRate = x
	x equals the speed you want; you can have it very specific, f.e. 1,15312 still works
Close the Console with CTRL + SHIFT + J or COMMAND + SHIFT + C.

Download playlists with idm:
	-go to "https://youtubemultidownloader.net/"
	-click playlist
	-paste the link
	-click download
	-copy the long list it gives you
	-open idm
	-click "tasks"
	-click "add batch download from clipboard"
	-select the ones you wanna download and hit ok.
